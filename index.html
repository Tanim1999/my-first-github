The laws of stoics

  • Stoics never rely on luck.
  • Stoics rely on their hard work.
  • stoics don't expect a work to be easy. No matter how hard the work is they keep doing the work until they become successful.
  • Their brain is wired such a way that if they want something they just get it.
  • They are totally aware of the fact that hard work beats talent when talent doesn't hard work.
  • Stoics always look at the bigger picture and avoid temporary comforts.
  • A stoic will take the hardship on his shoulder so that his family can live comfortably
  • Stoics compete with self only. They believe in getting better than yesterday.
  • stoics are always patient about rewards.They don't pay attention to rewards. Instead they put attention in the process and they know the reward is just a consequence.
  • stoics need no motivation to work. If there is something that will make them better than yesterday they will just do it.

Don't run away from pain. Embrace it, cause the only thing it will do for you is make you stronger!
